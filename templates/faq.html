Safety & Security
We verify identities via government ID uploads, but we do not conduct full criminal background checks. Users are advised to exercise caution.
For immediate emergencies, please dial 100 (Police) or 112. For platform issues, contact our support team.
We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment. Report any abusive behavior immediately, and we will take strict action, including account bans.
Yes, chats between users are private. However, they may be reviewed by moderators if a report is filed.
Payments & Refunds
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking via our secure payment gateway.
Refunds for cancelled bookings (if eligible) are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
Security deposits are held by the owner and are refundable at the end of the tenancy, subject to property condition and terms.
Payment failures can happen due to network issues or bank downtime. Please try again or contact your bank.
