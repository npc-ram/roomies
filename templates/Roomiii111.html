About Roomies

Roomies is India's most trusted student housing platform, connecting college students with verified, affordable accommodations near their campuses. Founded by students, for students, we understand the unique challenges of finding safe, comfortable housing while pursuing your education.

Our mission is to eliminate the stress and uncertainty from student housing by providing a transparent, secure platform where students can find their perfect home and compatible roommates through our AI-powered matching system.

With over 10,000 verified listings across 50+ cities and partnerships with leading colleges, Roomies has helped thousands of students find their ideal living situation. Every property is personally verified, every owner is background-checked, and every transaction is secured through our escrow system.
